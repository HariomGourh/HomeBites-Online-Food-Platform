About HomeBites.com

HomeBites is a platform that connects passionate home chefs with hungry customers looking for fresh, delicious, and authentic homemade meals. Inspired by platforms like Swiggy and Zomato, we bring the warmth of home-cooked food right to your doorstep.

We empower local cooks to showcase their culinary talents and earn from their kitchens. Whether you're craving North Indian thalis, South Indian dosas, Bengali sweets, or fusion delights, HomeBites has something for everyone – prepared with love and served with hygiene.

Why choose HomeBites?

  • ✔ 100% homemade food
  • ✔ Verified local home chefs
  • ✔ Safe, clean & contactless delivery
  • ✔ Affordable prices
  • ✔ Support local talent and women entrepreneurs

Join us in making homemade food more accessible, sustainable, and enjoyable for all!
